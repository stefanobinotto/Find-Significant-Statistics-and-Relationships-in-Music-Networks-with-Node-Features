
+-------------------------------------------------+
|     Genre Collaboration Network - Original	  |
+-------------------------------------------------+

The networks of genres obtained from Spotify Charts for each considered market and year. There is an edge between two genres when when artists belonging to these genres are linked (i.e. they collaborate on a hit song).

Genres in these networks are those provided by Spotify for each artist.
